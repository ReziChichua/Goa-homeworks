Classic Margherita Pizza

A timeless Italian recipe that brings together fresh ingredients and bold flavors.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough and roll it out.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
